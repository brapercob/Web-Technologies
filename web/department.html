History - About Our University

History of our University

The department was founded in 1989, together with the Institute of Informatics, as the Department of Informatics. In 1995, at the same time as the Department of Applied Informatics was established, the department took on the name of the Department of General Informatics. Until June 2007, the head of the department dr. It was Dénes Vadász, then the head of the department was dr. László Kovács took over. The basic reason for establishing the department coincided with the reason for founding the Institute: organizational framework, thus creating an opportunity to introduce and deepen the knowledge of IT sciences in the curricula of various undergraduate and postgraduate courses, to create the IT specialization of mechanical engineering.

Our department is currently in charge of the expiring university-level technical informatics program and in it the S8 application development specialization. We have significant subjects in the curriculum of the outgoing college-level programming mathematics program, the university-level economics programming mathematics program, the university-level technical manager program in the field of informatics. In the three IT courses of the BSc program (engineering, economics and programming informatics), we teach the defining subjects of the IT core material, and we also play a significant role in the accredited engineering and planned master's programs in business informatics.
